Why Choose Linen

Eco-Friendly

Linen is made from flax, a natural fiber that requires minimal water and pesticides to grow.

Hypoallergenic

Naturally resistant to bacteria and fungi, making it perfect for sensitive skin.

Thermo-Regulating

Keeps you cool in summer and warm in winter by adapting to your body temperature.

Moisture Absorbent

Can absorb up to 20% of its weight in moisture before feeling damp.

Highly Durable

Linen is one of the strongest natural fibers, becoming softer and more lustrous with each wash.

Sustainable

Biodegradable and recyclable, linen has a minimal environmental footprint.
